## Quick Reference: All Required Courses for IS Majors

The following courses are **mandatory** for all BS in Information Systems students (regardless of concentration):

1. **67-100** - Information Systems First Year Colloquium
2. **67-250** - The Information Systems Milieux
3. **67-262** - Database Design and Development
4. **67-272** - Application Design and Development
5. **17-313** - Foundations of Software Engineering
6. **67-373** - Information Systems Consulting Project
7. One Mathematics course (21-112, 21-120, 21-127, 21-240, or 80-210)
8. Three Computer Science courses (must include either 15-121 or 15-122)
9. One HCI course
10. One Digital Transformation course
11. One Professional Communications course
12. One Quantitative Analysis course
13. One Innovation/Entrepreneurship course
14. Concentration courses (36 units)
