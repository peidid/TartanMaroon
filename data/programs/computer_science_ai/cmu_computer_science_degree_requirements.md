## Program Requirements Summary

- **Total Units Required to Graduate:** 360 units
- **Mode/Average Units per Course:** 9 units

### Double Counting Rules

- **General Rule:** Courses may not double count with other programs unless specified otherwise

### Double Counting Exceptions

**Unlimited Double Counting Allowed:**
- Humanities and Arts
- Mathematics and Probability
- Science and Engineering

**Limited Double Counting:**
- **2 SCS Electives and Computer Science:** Maximum 5 courses

## Quick Reference: All Required Courses for CS Majors

The following courses or course selections are **mandatory** for all BS in Computer Science students:

### Computer Science (Required)
1. **15-122** - Principles of Imperative Computation
2. **15-150** - Principles of Functional Programming
3. **15-210** - Parallel and Sequential Data Structures and Algorithms
4. **15-251** - Great Ideas in Theoretical Computer Science
5. **15-451** - Algorithm Design and Analysis
6. One Introduction to Computer Systems course (15-213 or 18-213)
7. One Algorithms and Complexity elective
8. One Logics and Languages elective
9. One Software Systems elective
10. One Applications elective
11. Two SCS Electives

### Mathematics (Required)
1. **21-120** - Differential and Integral Calculus
2. **21-122** - Integration and Approximation
3. One Concepts of Mathematics course (minimum grade C)
4. One Matrix and Linear Algebra course
5. One Probability course

### Humanities and Arts (Required)
1. One Interpretation and Argument course
2. One Qatar Topics course
3. Three Humanities and Arts electives

### Science and Engineering (Required)
1. Four Science/Engineering courses from any approved department
2. Two Science/Engineering courses from the same department
3. One Laboratory course

### Other Requirements
1. One Freshman Immigration course
2. One Computing at Carnegie Mellon course
3. Three Free Electives
