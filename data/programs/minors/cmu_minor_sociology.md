## Quick Reference: Summary of Minor Requirements

### Total Courses Required: 6 courses minimum

1. **Core Course:** 1 course
   - 70-311 (Organizational Behavior)

2. **Methodology:** 1 course
   - Choose from: 36-202, 70-208, 85-340, 88-251, or 88-252

   - At least 1 from Category 1 (Sociology of Gender, Family, and Culture)
   - At least 1 from Category 2 (Sociology of Work, Organizations, and Technology)
   - Remaining 2 courses can be from either category

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many courses do I need for the Sociology minor?
You need a minimum of 6 courses: 1 core course (70-311), 1 methodology course, and at least 4 elective courses distributed across two categories.

### Can I double count courses?
Yes. Up to 2 courses may double count with other programs. Additionally, you may double count without restrictions with specific General Education programs (Tepper Core, Dietrich Gen Ed, MCS Gen Ed, and SCS Gen Ed for EY2021+).

### What if I want to take more than the minimum electives?
You may take additional elective courses beyond the minimum 4 required. The requirement specifies "at least 4 courses" from the elective categories.

### Can I take all 4 electives from one category?
No. You must take at least 1 course from Category 1 (Gender, Family, and Culture) AND at least 1 course from Category 2 (Work, Organizations, and Technology). The remaining 2 electives can be from either category.

### Are there other courses that count toward the minor?
Yes. The program notes that "other pre-approved courses may also count" for the electives. Consult with your academic advisor for pre-approval of courses not listed.

### What is the minimum number of units required?
The minor requires a minimum of 54 units total, typically achieved through 6 courses at 9 units each.
