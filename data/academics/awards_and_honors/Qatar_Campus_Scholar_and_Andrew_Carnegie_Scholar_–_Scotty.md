### **Qatar Campus Scholar Awardees**

Four students are chosen from the senior class as **Qatar Campus Scholar**.  They represent the best intersection of academic excellence, contributions to the community, volunteerism, and the many forms of student leadership that are the hallmarks of a Carnegie Mellon alum.

The students will be presented with a stole to wear with their regalia.

#### Selection Criteria

Eligible students are invited to apply:

GPA 3.75 or above.

The selected student should “embody high standards of academic excellence combined with multi-dimensional characteristics such as volunteerism, involvement in student organizations, participation in sports or the arts (other than for academic credit). In addition, the program is designed to specifically include those students who demonstrate strong leadership skills” (ACS Guidelines).

No Academic Integrity Violations (Exceptions: minor violation on the first year)

### **The Andrew Carnegie Scholar (ACS)**

**This is an annual award that is given to one senior from our campus.**

The Andrew Carnegie Scholar embodies high standards of academic excellence combined with multi-dimensional characteristics s_uch as volunteerism, involvement in student organizations, participation in sports or the arts (other than for academic credit)_. The chosen student also demonstrates strong leadership skills. The student is chosen amongst the four Qatar Campus Scholars.

The student will be presented with a stole to wear with the regalia.
